Our Story

We've always known that there is much more to Butlers than merely chivalry and etiquette. But how could we know there’s THIS MUCH more?


Hello World!Meet Butlers for Humanity NFT project.
Butlers are an often overlooked class of people, despite their essential roles in the lives of the wealthy. They keep everything running, are there when you need them, and then seem to fade into the background when you don't!

Little did you know that all butlers lead double lives: they're all a part of a secret society, trained to benefit the whole of humanity from the background and never raising a fuss.

Until now, they've been able to do their work quietly and avoid being on the radar, but that's all about to change. As the evil world continues to evolve, a more direct approach is required: and the Butlers for Humanity are prepared to meet it head-on in Web 3.

Complete with bow ties, fancy facial hair, and white gloves, 10,000 Butlers for Humanity are ready to live on Ethereum blockchain and be of service anywhere at any time.

Every one of these butlers has gone to an esteemed butler school that trains them not only in the skills of poise and service, but also in combat and stealth.

They'll make an amazing addition to the wallet that they're assigned to, and their dapper service is sure to delight.

It’s time for the Butlers to take the lead, spearheading the fight for humanity. Join us!

UTILITY

This project is all about doing good and serving up humanitarian efforts in the face of evil.

That's why we've already begun creating a 3D game (see "GAME" section for more details) for our Butlers to showcase their normally-hidden skills. They're not afraid to get their hands dirty but they always keep their gloves clean!

Each Butler holds a tray on which items of varying rarity are contained.

There will be 500 Butlers holding golden Ethereum on their trays (5% rarity).

Butlers are constructed out of more than 400+ traits … making each one a rare character by himself! The main body of Butlers & the tray content will only reveal itself after that particular Butler has been minted to a wallet.

Butlers are always ready to serve!
Butlers are a tight-knit community with a focus on doing good and being of service to humanity.

We will be donating 20% of all proceeds (including future royalties) to: Action Against Hunger Canada And The Hunger Project

Furthermore, the community of token holders can vote on a charity to support with financial contribution (as long as it's a non-profit, and accepts Crypto donations)

Both organizations above are accepting Crypto … so donations will be 100% transparent on the blockchain.
